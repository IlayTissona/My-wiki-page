What is Full-on psytrance?

Full-On trance is a psychedelic trance style which has high energy for peak moments, often having melodic, energetic, and crisp basslines with a fast tempo (usually 140-148 bpm).

Main charachters of full-on psytrance
  • High energy for peak moments.
  • Often having melodic, energetic, and crisp basslines with a fast tempo (usually 140-148 bpm).
  • There are some related styles that are derived from this style and are distinguished as different varieties of Full-On: twilight and night full-on (or dark full-on), having bolder and lower notes in their basslines, morning (light and kind of happy), and uplifting
  • High energy smile forcing music
