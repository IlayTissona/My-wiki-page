Dark-Psytrance

The heavy metal of psytrance

What is dark-psytrance?

Dark psychedelic (also dark psytrance, darkpsy or dark-trance) trance is the heavier end of the psychedelic trance spectrum with tempos around 148 bpm and over. Related styles include psycore (fast and crazy), hi-tech (bouncy and glitchy), and forest (organic and earthy).

Main charachters of dark psytrance
  • Obscure, deep, and more eschatological background that leads into profound meditation of death, night, and transcendence.
  • Often comes with dismal sounds and heavy basslines.
  • The subgenre often samples horror films in contrast to the science fiction film samples more regularly used in "normal" psytrance.
  • Much like heavy metal - dark psytrance gives an angry vibe of murder and\or breaking things.
