Spiritual awakening, hypnotic melodic sound

What is goa psytrance?

Goa trance is an electronic music style that originated during the late 1980s in Goa, India (as can be guessed by its name). Goa trance is the first of all psytrance subgenres, this is what started it all.

Main charachters of goa-psytrance
  • Goa trance often has funky, drone-like basslines.
  • The kick drum often is a low, thick sound with prominent sub-bass frequencies.
  • A popular element of Goa trance is the use of vocal samples, often from science fiction movies.
